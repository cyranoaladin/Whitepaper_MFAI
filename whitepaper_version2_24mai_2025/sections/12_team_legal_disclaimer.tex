\section{Team \& Legal Disclaimer}

\subsection{Team}

\begin{mfai-box}{Team Structure Overview}{users}
The \mfaiprotocol{Team Structure} defines the organizational framework and roles within the MoneyFactory AI ecosystem.
\end{mfai-box}

Key roles:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item \mfaihighlight{Core Team}: Protocol development
\item \mfaihighlight{Technical Team}: System architecture
\item \mfaihighlight{Research Team}: Innovation focus
\item \mfaihighlight{Community Team}: Builder support
\item \mfaihighlight{Operations Team}: Protocol management
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Legal Structure}

\begin{mfai-box-learn}{Legal Framework Overview}{balance-scale}
The \mfaiprotocol{Legal Structure} establishes the legal framework and compliance requirements for the MoneyFactory AI ecosystem.
\end{mfai-box-learn}

Compliance areas:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item \mfaihighlight{Securities Laws}: Regulatory compliance
\item \mfaihighlight{Financial Regulations}: Market operations
\item \mfaihighlight{Data Protection}: Privacy standards
\item \mfaihighlight{Anti-Money Laundering}: Security measures
\item \mfaihighlight{Consumer Protection}: Builder rights
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Risk Management}

\begin{mfai-box-build}{Risk Management System}{shield-alt}
The \mfaiprotocol{Risk Framework} outlines the risk management strategies and mitigation measures for the MoneyFactory AI ecosystem.
\end{mfai-box-build}

Risk categories:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item \mfaihighlight{Legal Risks}: Regulatory compliance
\item \mfaihighlight{Operational Risks}: System security
\item \mfaihighlight{Market Risks}: Economic factors
\item \mfaihighlight{Compliance Risks}: Regulatory changes
\item \mfaihighlight{Reputation Risks}: Public perception
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Legal Disclaimers}

\begin{mfai-box-prove}{Legal Disclaimers Overview}{exclamation-triangle}

Disclaimer types:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
